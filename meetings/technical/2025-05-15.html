2025-05-15

AID2E bi-weekly technical meeting

Meeting: May 15, 2025

Attendees: Amit Bashyal, Fang-Ying Tsai, Meifeng Lin, Tianle Wang, Wen Guan, Gabor Galgoczi, Connor Pecar, Anselm Vossen, Derek Anderson, Cristiano Fanelli, Karthik Suresh, Hemalata Nayak
  • Announcements
    • Derek Anderson was welcomed to the collaboration.
    • The second quarterly report had been submitted.
  • Agenda
    • Documentation updates from W&M, BNL, and Duke were discussed.
    • Holistic optimization focused on making ePIC objectives generic enough for detector response.
    • dRICH updates covered feedback from the dRICH group, design-space complexity, and infrastructure needs.
    • Forward-disk optimization and far-forward integration were discussed.
    • Other applications included optimization of an iron/scintillator sandwich calorimeter.
    • PanDA dRICH work continued with multiple-file support and debugging.
  • Action items
    • Increase complexity for the stress-test exercise.
    • Investigate integrated central plus far-forward reactions.
    • Share the Overleaf for the Computing Software and Big Science paper.
