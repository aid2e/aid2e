AID2E bi-weekly technical meeting

Meeting: August 12, 2025

Attendees: Amit Bashyal, Meifeng Lin, Tianle Wang, Torre Wenaus, Tanja Horn, Baptiste Fraisse, Derek Anderson, Karthik Suresh, Hemalata Nayak
  • Announcements: none.
  • Agenda
    • Amit noted Fang was on holiday and Wen could not join live.
    • Wen updates included concurrent trials, checkpoint/restart, timing metrics, and hypervolume support.
    • Fang reported that standalone hypervolume calculations were consistent with dRICH-MOBO.
    • Amit was curating multi-file dRICH workflows and requested maintainer access to scheduler_epic.

    • Meifeng summarized Tianle's PanDA harvester and Perlmutter submission work.
    • Perlmutter usage was encouraged.
    • A production-stage resource proposal was discussed, with an October 6 deadline.
  • Reminder on the problem
    • The team discussed which subsystems to prioritize and the role of holistic optimization.
