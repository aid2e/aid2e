2023-11-07

AID2E bi-weekly general meeting

Meeting: November 7, 2023

Attendees: Anselm Vossen, Connor Pecar, Cristiano Fanelli, Karthik Suresh, Markus Diefenthaler, Meifeng Lin, Tanja Horn, Torre Wenaus, Tianle Wang
  • Documentation and collaboration
    • The group discussed repository documentation, collaboration policy, and lightweight contributor expectations.
  • Technical progress
    • Closure Test 1 progress was reviewed through the WandB dashboard.
    • Closure Test 2 work toward running containerized workloads through PanDA also continued.
  • Detector targets
    • The group debated whether to begin with ePIC sub-detectors or with simpler second-detector examples.
    • Far Forward and dRICH emerged as the most realistic first ePIC targets.
    • The team also discussed possible cost objectives and future larger-scale optimization problems.
